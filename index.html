The Concept

FAQ

Questions? Answers.

Do I need coding experience?

No! That's the whole point. Vibe coding is about using AI tools to build without traditional coding skills. We'll teach you everything you need to know.

What should I bring?

Your laptop, charger, and an idea (even a half-baked one). We'll provide the tools, mentors, and environment to turn it into something real.

Who is this for?

Women, non-binary folks, and allies who want to build with AI. Whether you're a founder, creative, operator, or just curious — this is your space.

Will there be food and drinks?

Yes, complimentary drinks and light bites throughout the evening. We keep you fueled while you build.

Can I come alone?

Absolutely. You'll leave with a crew. The energy in the room makes it impossible not to connect.
